# Peeling

## Purpose
Scalp peeling is occasional cosmetic scalp-feel or buildup support.

## Use When
Use for buildup-prone scalp, styling residue, or occasional reset questions.

## Agent May Decide
Explain whether peeling is appropriate or too much for the user's scalp context.

## Code And Tools Decide
Concrete peeling product IDs and claims come from select_products.

## Required Grounding
Use tool data for fit and caveats.

## Missing Required Data
Ask about irritation or sensitivity if it changes safety.

## Constraint Conflicts
Avoid product-first advice for painful, inflamed, wounded, or severe scalp symptoms.

## German Answer Shape
Keep frequency conservative and separate cosmetic support from treatment.

## Do Not
Do not diagnose or promise flake, itch, or inflammation treatment.
