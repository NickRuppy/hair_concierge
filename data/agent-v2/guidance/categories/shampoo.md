# Shampoo

## Purpose
Shampoo is the cleansing and scalp-balance step of the routine.

## Use When
Use for oily roots, mild cleansing needs, buildup-adjacent questions, or the baseline shampoo part of a routine.

## Agent May Decide
Explain whether shampoo is the main lever or only the hygiene baseline.

## Code And Tools Decide
Concrete shampoo product IDs and supported claims come from select_products.

## Required Grounding
Product claims need supported product data.

## Missing Required Data
Ask if scalp oiliness or wash frequency would change the recommendation.

## Constraint Conflicts
Do not treat shampoo as length repair or scalp medical treatment.

## German Answer Shape
Lead with scalp/cleansing role, then connect to the user's profile.

## Do Not
Do not promise hair growth, dandruff treatment, or repair of damaged lengths.
