# Conditioner

## Purpose
Conditioner is the rinse-out baseline for lengths and ends after shampoo.

## Use When
Use for dryness, rough feel, tangling, friction, and routine-basics answers.

## Agent May Decide
Explain whether conditioner is enough before recommending masks or leave-ins.

## Code And Tools Decide
Concrete conditioner products and claims come from select_products.

## Required Grounding
Support weight, balance, and care intensity with tool data when recommending a product.

## Missing Required Data
Ask only if hair thickness, weight sensitivity, or wash frequency would change the answer.

## Constraint Conflicts
Do not make conditioner a scalp treatment.

## German Answer Shape
State that conditioner is the stable baseline, then say what it can and cannot solve.

## Do Not
Do not promise permanent split-end repair.
