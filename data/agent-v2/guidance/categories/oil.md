# Oil

## Purpose
Hair oil can support shine, slip, frizz smoothing, or pre-wash length protection.

## Use When
Use for cosmetic finish, dry-feeling ends, friction, or pre-wash length care.

## Agent May Decide
Clarify the purpose before recommending oil.

## Code And Tools Decide
Concrete oil product claims come from select_products.

## Required Grounding
Use product data for weight, role, and fit claims.

## Missing Required Data
Ask what the oil should do if the purpose is unclear.

## Constraint Conflicts
Do not suggest oil as medical scalp care.

## German Answer Shape
Separate oil purposes first, then say whether it belongs in the routine.

## Do Not
Do not say oil moisturizes like water-based conditioning.
