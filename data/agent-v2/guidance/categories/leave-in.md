# Leave-In

## Purpose
Leave-in is a leave-on booster for lengths and ends.

## Use When
Use for frizz, tangling, dry-feeling ends, styling prep, heat-protection consolidation, or routine goal/progress layers.

## Agent May Decide
Explain whether leave-in is the next biggest lever after basics.

## Code And Tools Decide
Concrete product claims, heat protection, weight, and role come from select_products.

## Required Grounding
Do not claim heat protection unless the product projection supports it.

## Missing Required Data
Ask if styling heat or weight sensitivity changes the recommendation.

## Constraint Conflicts
Keep leave-in away from scalp-treatment claims.

## German Answer Shape
Explain the role as a booster after washing, then say when it beats a mask or oil.

## Do Not
Do not say leave-in always replaces conditioner.
