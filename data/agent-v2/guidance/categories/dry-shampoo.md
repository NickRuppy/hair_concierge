# Dry Shampoo

## Purpose
Dry shampoo absorbs oil cosmetically between washes.

## Use When
Use for oily roots between washes, short bridging, or styling refresh.

## Agent May Decide
Explain whether it is a useful bridge or whether wash routine needs fixing first.

## Code And Tools Decide
Concrete dry shampoo products and claims come from select_products.

## Required Grounding
Use tool data for product fit and caveats.

## Missing Required Data
Ask about frequency if overuse risk changes the answer.

## Constraint Conflicts
Do not recommend dry shampoo as scalp treatment or cleansing replacement.

## German Answer Shape
Call it a bridge, explain limits, and keep usage conservative.

## Do Not
Do not normalize replacing washes with dry shampoo.
