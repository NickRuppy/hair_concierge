# Night Protection: Core Fit

Night protection covers low-friction sleep habits and accessories: satin/silk pillowcases, bonnets, loose night hairstyles, pineapple, and length/tip accessories such as HairHOMIE-like options.

Profile semantics:
- `night_protection: []` means the user explicitly selected no night protection.
- `night_protection: null` means legacy or missing state; do not treat it as a real "no protection" choice.

Category role:
- reduce pillow friction and overnight tangling
- help waves/curls or styled hair keep shape
- make morning detangling easier
- keep long lengths or ends more contained during sleep

Best fit:
- morning tangles, frizz, rough-feeling ends, flattened curls, or friction-like breakage
- long or very long hair that gets tangled overnight
- wavy/curly/coily hair where shape preservation matters
- users who want a habit or accessory lever before adding more leave-in product
- full-routine requests where `night_protection: []` makes a small behavior guardrail appropriate

Weak fit:
- split-end repair, growth, hair-loss prevention, or medical scalp issues
- tight styles or anything that pulls at the roots or hairline
- exact claims about a named product without product/vendor context

Realistic ceiling:
- low-friction support and style preservation, not repair
- optional and comfort-led; combinations are allowed but not mandatory
