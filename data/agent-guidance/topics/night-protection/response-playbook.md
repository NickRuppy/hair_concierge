# Night Protection: Response Playbook

Default answer shape:
- name the likely mechanism: overnight friction, tangling, or style disruption
- suggest one lowest-burden option first
- explain why it fits the user's length, texture, or symptom pattern
- keep combinations optional rather than mandatory

Proactive trigger:
- mention night protection proactively when `night_protection: []` combines with breakage, split ends, hair damage, tangling, frizz, rough ends, morning tangles, long/very long hair, curl definition, anti-breakage or strengthening goals, or a full-routine request where behavior guardrails are appropriate
- treat `night_protection: null` as missing/legacy state and ask or answer conditionally instead of assuming the user has no protection

Option selection:
- satin/silk pillowcase: passive baseline, good if the user dislikes headwear, combines with other options
- bonnet: broader containment, useful for curls/coils or defined styles when fit is comfortable and not tight
- loose tied hair: medium to very long hair that tangles when fully loose; use soft tie and low tension
- pineapple: wavy/curly medium-long to long hair where curl shape or volume preservation is the goal
- length/tip accessory: long or very long hair where lengths/ends tangle; HairHOMIE can be described as this accessory type unless exact product claims are requested

Good reasons to ask one follow-up:
- hair length is unknown and would change the option
- the user may not tolerate bonnets or headwear
- the user already uses one option but still has symptoms
- the question is about exact HairHOMIE product claims rather than the accessory concept

Useful answer-safe phrasing:
- "Das ist eher ein Reibungs-Thema als ein Repair-Thema."
- "Wenn du nachts noch nichts nutzt, wäre ein Satin-/Seidenkissenbezug der niedrigste Aufwand."
- "Bei langen Haaren und verknoteten Spitzen kann ein Längen-/Spitzen-Schutz wie HairHOMIE sinnvoller sein als nur lockeres Zusammenbinden."
- "Wenn du schon ein Bonnet nutzt, würde ich erst Sitz und Spannung prüfen, bevor wir noch einen Schritt ergänzen."
