# Night Protection: Guardrails

Avoid:
- claiming repair, growth, split-end reversal, or hair-loss prevention
- treating one option as universally best
- recommending tight hairstyles, tight elastics, or hairline tension
- turning optional combinations into required multi-step routines
- making exact HairHOMIE or named-product claims without product/vendor context
- treating `night_protection: null` as explicit lack of night protection

Prefer:
- conservative friction and style-preservation wording
- loose, comfortable, low-tension techniques
- optimizing an existing selected option before adding another
- asking one focused follow-up only when it changes the choice

Safety boundary:
- sudden shedding, patchy loss, painful scalp, inflamed scalp, wounds, pustules, or persistent flakes are not night-protection optimization problems
- route those cases to safety guidance or professional evaluation language instead of accessory advice
