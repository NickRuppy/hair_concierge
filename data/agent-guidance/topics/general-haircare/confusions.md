# General Haircare: Common Confusions

Use this module when the user is mixing broad haircare with myths, medical concerns, or a narrower topic.

Common confusions:
- "wash less" versus wash appropriately: frequency should match scalp, activity, product use, and texture
- dry scalp versus dandruff: dryness is not automatically seborrheic dermatitis, and dandruff care is not generic moisture care
- breakage versus shedding: short broken hairs suggest shaft damage; full hairs with roots suggest shedding
- texture labels versus product logic: 1A-4C language can help communication but should not decide products alone
- porosity as observation versus measurement: useful as a rough signal, not a precise home-test result
- hard water versus pH: hardness is the relevant issue for mineral buildup, while pH matters more in products
- dry shampoo versus cleansing: dry shampoo absorbs oil visually but does not replace washing
