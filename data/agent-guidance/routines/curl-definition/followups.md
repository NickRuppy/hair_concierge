# Curl Definition Routine: Followups

Ask at most one follow-up unless the user explicitly wants a full intake.

High-value follow-ups:
- "Do you want your wave or curl pattern to show, or do you prefer smoother low-maintenance hair?"
- "Is your hair wavy, curly, or coily?"
- "Is your hair fine, normal, or coarse?"
- "What is your scalp doing right now: fast-greasing, dry or itchy, oily with flakes, calm, or inflamed/painful?"
- "Any colour, bleach, relaxer, keratin, heavy heat, breakage, or mushy wet feel in the last six months?"
- "Does your current routine hold definition, or does it collapse, frizz, feel dry, or feel flat?"
- "How often do you realistically wash and style?"
- "Is your climate very humid or very dry?"

Skip follow-ups that do not change routine assembly, such as fragrance preference, brand loyalty, or exact price tier, unless the user has moved into product selection.
