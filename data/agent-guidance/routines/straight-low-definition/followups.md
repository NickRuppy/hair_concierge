# Straight And Low-Definition Wavy Routine: Followups

Ask at most one follow-up unless the user explicitly wants a full intake.

High-value follow-ups:
- "What is your scalp doing right now: fast-greasing, dry or itchy, oily with flakes, calm, or inflamed/painful?"
- "Any bleach, colour, keratin, perm, relaxer, or heavy heat in the last six months?"
- "Does the hair break on brushing, feel mushy when wet, snap fast, or mostly feel balanced?"
- "Is your hair fine, normal, or coarse?"
- "Do you want smoother low-maintenance waves, or do you want curl/wave definition?"
- "Is your current routine working, and if not, what specifically changed?"
- "How much routine time will you realistically do?"

Skip follow-ups that do not change routine assembly, such as fragrance preference, brand loyalty, or exact price tier, unless the user has moved into product selection.
