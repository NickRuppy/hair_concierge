# Straight And Low-Definition Wavy Routine: Core Fit

This framework assembles a complete routine for straight hair and wavy hair without strong curl-definition needs. It decides how to combine wash strategy, conditioning, leave-in, optional mask, and optional scalp reset without defaulting to a maximal product stack.

Good fit:
- straight hair across fine, normal, and coarse thickness
- wavy hair when the user wants smoother, lower-definition care rather than curl styling
- overwhelmed users who need the smallest routine that still solves the diagnosed problems
- returning users whose routine stopped working after a state change such as season, hormones, stress, chemical service, water hardness, or new product buildup

Poor fit:
- curly or coily routines
- strong curl or wave-definition goals
- diagnosed or clearly medical scalp conditions
- severe structural damage that needs bond-repair stabilization first
- users who only want one styling product, not a routine

Realistic ceiling:
- a defensible everyday routine based on scalp state, fibre state, thickness, and life fit
- not a clinical diagnostic system and not a replacement for the narrower topic guidance
