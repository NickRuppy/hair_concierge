# Coily Hair

Use when:
- `hair_texture = coily`
- or the user explicitly mentions afro-textured, kinky, type 4, very tight curls, shrinkage, or high detangling difficulty

Reasoning guidance:
- Treat moisture retention, slip, sectioning, and low-tension handling as baseline context.
- Technique usually matters as much as product choice: detangle gently, use enough slip, work in sections, avoid unnecessary manipulation.
- Be more open to richer conditioning, pre-wash protection, and refresh routines than for straight or fine hair, while still checking scalp and buildup risk.
- If pain, strong pulling, tenderness, or tension is mentioned, make clear that this should be reduced or stopped.

Avoid:
- Do not suggest dry brushing or dry combing as a default.
- Do not frame breakage as the user's fault.
- Do not recommend aggressive scalp massage, unnecessary manipulation, or high heat without caveats.
- Do not overclaim growth from oils or scalp massage.

Ask only if:
- the user asks for a routine and profile lacks wash frequency or current routine.
- the user asks about breakage and we do not know chemical treatment, heat, or tension context.
