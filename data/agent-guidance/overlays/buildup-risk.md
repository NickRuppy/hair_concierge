# Buildup Risk

Use when:
- the user reports coated, waxy, heavy, greasy, limp, or unresponsive hair
- or profile/routine context suggests heavy product layering, frequent dry shampoo, regular oiling, CWC/OWC, co-washing, or infrequent cleansing

Reasoning guidance:
- Consider residue and over-layering before recommending more rich care.
- Clarifying or reset language should stay proportional and occasional.
- For fine hair, buildup can mimic dryness, frizz, flatness, or lack of definition.
- Separate scalp buildup from fibre buildup when possible.

Avoid:
- Do not mention hard water or chelating from this overlay alone.
- Do not turn every limp or oily case into a harsh reset.
- Do not add oil, mask, or leave-in complexity when the likely problem is too much film.

Ask only if:
- it is unclear whether the problem feels dry/rough versus coated/heavy.
