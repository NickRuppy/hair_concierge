# Damage Repair

Use when:
- bleach, oxidative color, relaxer, perm, keratin treatment, repeated hot tools, breakage, mushy wet feel, high elasticity, or rough fragile lengths are relevant

Reasoning guidance:
- Separate structural damage from dryness and buildup.
- Bond builders, protein, conditioning, and behavior changes have different jobs; do not flatten them into "repair."
- Repair products can improve strength, feel, and resilience, but cannot restore hair to virgin condition.
- Split ends need trimming; surrounding hair can still be protected and strengthened.

Avoid:
- Do not promise permanent repair, regrowth, or split-end reversal.
- Do not recommend bond builders for every dry or frizzy complaint.
- Do not ignore heat and mechanical behavior when damage is ongoing.

Ask only if:
- the answer depends on whether damage is chemical, heat-driven, mechanical, or mostly dryness.
