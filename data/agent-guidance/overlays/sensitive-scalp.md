# Sensitive Scalp

Use when:
- the user mentions itch, redness, irritation, burning, pain, tenderness, sores, persistent inflammation, or active flaky symptoms

Reasoning guidance:
- Keep scalp safety above cosmetic product optimization.
- Prefer gentle, low-irritation advice and fewer aggressive steps.
- Pause oils, scrubs, aggressive exfoliation, harsh scalp tools, and strong fragrance/active stacking when irritation is active.
- Distinguish cosmetic discomfort from medical-adjacent symptoms without diagnosing.

Avoid:
- Do not treat painful, inflamed, weeping, pustular, or patchy-loss scalp as a routine problem.
- Do not recommend aggressive scalp massage or exfoliation.
- Do not make simple dandruff the whole overlay; use `overlay:dandruff_scalp` when dandruff is explicit.

Ask only if:
- symptoms could be dry scalp, dandruff, allergy/irritation, or another scalp issue and the distinction changes the next step.
