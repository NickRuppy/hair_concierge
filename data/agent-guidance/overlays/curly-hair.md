# Curly Hair

Use when:
- `hair_texture = curly`
- or the user explicitly mentions curls, curl definition, refresh, clumping, shrinkage, or curl-related frizz

Reasoning guidance:
- Treat definition, slip, and friction control as first-class goals.
- Prefer technique-first advice before adding richer products.
- For refresh questions, think reactivate and reshape before adding more product.
- If `overlay:fine_hair` also applies, preserve curl support while avoiding heavy, flattening products.

Avoid:
- Do not assume curly hair automatically needs heavy masks or oils.
- Do not recommend brushing dry curls as a default.
- Do not treat frizz as always dryness; it can be technique, damage, humidity, buildup, or insufficient hold.
- Do not drift into hold gels or styling-product layering unless the user asks for styling.

Ask only if:
- the answer depends on whether the user wants definition, less frizz, easier detangling, or refresh between washes.
